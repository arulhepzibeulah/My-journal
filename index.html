Food

Perfect red velvet brownie

Red velvet brownies are a rich, cocoa-infused delight with a soft, velvety texture. Vibrant red hues and a swirl of cream cheese frosting make it irresistible—a perfect balance of sweetness and elegance in every bite.

Milk cake

Favourite, Popsicle

Travel

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace is a magnificent architectural marvel, showcasing grandeur and royal history. With its intricate carvings and stunning lighting, it's a must-visit for history enthusiasts and travelers.

Ooty

Ooty, nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, is a serene hill station known for its cool climate and scenic beauty. From lush tea gardens to tranquil lakes, it’s a perfect retreat for nature lovers.

The botanical garden

The Botanical Garden in Ooty is a lush paradise, featuring a vast collection of plants, flowers, and trees. A stroll through this garden is a peaceful experience for nature admirers.

Rubber Forest

The Rubber Forest in Kerala is a unique landscape filled with towering rubber trees. A walk through the forest offers a glimpse into the rubber-tapping process and the beauty of the natural surroundings.
